Objective: 
----------
To win Solitaire, you must get all the cards onto the Foundation piles. 
The Foundations are ordered by suit and rank, each Foundation has one 
suit and you must put the cards onto them in the order 
Ace 2 3 4 5 6 7 8 9 10 Jack Queen King. To get there, you can use the 
moves described below.

Piles:
------
The Stock: The pile of facedown cards in the upper left corner.
The Waste: The faceup pile next to the Stock in the upper left corner.
The Foundations: The four piles in the upper right corner.
The Tableau: The seven piles that make up the main table.

Moves:
------
1. Flip cards from the Stock onto the Waste.

2. Move a card from the Waste onto the Foundations.

3. Move a card from the Waste onto the Tableau.

4. Move a card from a Foundation back onto the Tableau. 

5. Move one or more cards from one Tableau pile to another pile with different coloured suit.

6. You can move a Tableau card onto the Foundations.

Score:
------
1. (+5) - Waste to Tableu
2. (+10) - Waste to Foundation
3. (+10) - Tableu to Foundation
4. (+5) - Turn over Tableu card
5. (-15) - Foundation to Tableu
6. (-100) - Recycle Waste (showing deck from the beginning)
7. (-2) - Every 30 seconds
